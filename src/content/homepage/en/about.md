Public Association "Counterpart-Sheriktesh" was founded in January 2002. The organization works in the field of strengthening civil society, protecting human rights, and democratic development of the Kyrgyz Republic.

Our mission is to promote the building of a democratic, law-governed state, develop civil society institutions, and protect human and civil rights and freedoms.

Over the years we have implemented dozens of projects aimed at monitoring human rights compliance, legal education of the population, development of local communities, and strengthening democratic institutions.
