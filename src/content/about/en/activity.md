To fulfill its mission, PA "Counterpart-Sheriktesh" provides services in the following areas:

**Organizational Development**
PA "Counterpart-Sheriktesh" provides technical support in organizational development to assist clients in solving identified problems that limit the effectiveness of their activities. Such problems require the involvement of qualified specialists working with the organization over an extended period.

**Training Programs/Research**
Training is an effective tool in the capacity building process of civil society actors. PA "Counterpart-Sheriktesh" pays special attention to training NGO representatives in advocacy skills and community mobilization for socially significant projects.

**Representative/Contract Services**
PA "Counterpart-Sheriktesh" can perform representative functions for international organizations that do not have local representation through grant program administration, providing information about their activities, technical assistance, and monitoring and evaluation of grants.

**Information Support**
PA "Counterpart-Sheriktesh" provides information support to CSO support centers and key NGOs, which relay information about grant and educational programs to beneficiaries.

**Social Partnership**
PA "Counterpart-Sheriktesh" facilitates intersectoral partnership for sustainable development of the third sector and the country as a whole. Partnerships provide NGOs with access to knowledge and skills; innovative and proven methodologies; reproducible models for meeting local community needs and shared resource management; various models of organizational management and leadership; strategies for protecting and promoting NGO rights and interests.

**Advocacy/Campaigns/Coalition Building**
PA "Counterpart-Sheriktesh" promotes the acquisition of advocacy skills for positive change in the interests of the most vulnerable populations, including public education and dialogue with authorities. Priority is given to projects and initiatives aimed at increasing civic participation in decision-making.

**Volunteerism**
PA "Counterpart-Sheriktesh" promotes the creation and development of a network of volunteer centers to advance the philosophy of volunteerism. An important component of the volunteer development program is engaging volunteers to implement the organization's project activities.

**Social Mobilization**
PA "Counterpart-Sheriktesh" promotes the concept of community self-reliance based on participatory approaches. It supports community initiatives through capacity building of community networks and coordination of joint efforts among organizations working in community development.

**Human Rights**
The human rights direction focuses on providing knowledge in international and national laws, human rights conventions, their promotion and protection, as well as mechanisms and procedures for their implementation, in order to strengthen respect for human rights. Knowledge in human rights helps civil society organizations and beneficiaries to act and contribute to improving the human rights situation in the country.

Thus, the strategy of PA "Counterpart-Sheriktesh" for capacity building of civil society actors represents an integrated package of services designed for non-profit organizations, public organizations, and other civil society entities.
