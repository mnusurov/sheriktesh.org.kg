**"Support to Refugee Women" and "Local Integration Support Program"**

Two UNHCR sub-projects are to be implemented by Public Association Counterpart-Sheriktesh in 2007. The project goal is to promote local integration of Tajik refugees and new citizens of Chui Oblast. The sub-project objectives and activities are:

1.  To facilitate acquisition of citizenship of the Kyrgyz Republic by Tajik refugees of Chui Oblast
2.  To identify needs of stateless persons of Chui Oblast
3.  To establish and develop 10 self-help groups (SHG) among refugees and new citizens in 5 pilot villages of Chui Oblast

**Type of services provided:**

-   To prepare passport applications by means of providing consultative support to refugee organizations
-   To provide citizenship grants to the refugee NGOs
-   To conduct survey among migrants in one pilot rayon
-   To develop survey with recommendation for future programming to assist stateless persons
-   To Assist CSSC of Kara-Balta, NGO Sairon, Adab and YryshKenchi in developing 4 new self-help groups among women
-   To provide technical assistance to SHGs
-   To monitor credit programme to SHGs etc.
