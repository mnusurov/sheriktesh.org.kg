<p><strong>Establishment of the Educational and Legal Centres in Chui and Jalalabat Oblasts of the Kyrgyz Republic</strong></p>
      <p>The project contributes to strengthening civil society organizations in improving situation with trafficking in women and children through strengthening capacity of 2 networks of NGOs of Chui and Jalalabat Oblasts working on the issue of trafficking women and children and refugees, strengthening capacity of civil society organizations (network NGOs) working on advocacy of vulnerable groups, particularly on Human Rights issues in Chui, Osh and Jalalabat oblasts of the Kyrgyz Republic.</p>
      <p><strong>Type of services provided:</strong></p>
      <ul>
      <li>Organizational capacity and service delivery of civil society organizations of network of refugee NGOs (seven NGOs) and network of anti trafficking NGOs (25 NGOs) in advocacy, promotion of human rights to protect interests and rights of their beneficiaries are strengthened</li>
      <li>Level of public awareness in combating trafficking in women and children is increased.</li>
      <li>Engagement of governmental officials in policy dialogue with citizens groups and NGOs are ensured.</li>
      </ul>
</p>
