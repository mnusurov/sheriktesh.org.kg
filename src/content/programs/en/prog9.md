<p>The objectives of the research: 

<ol><li>To gather and analyze data on cooperation projects and activities that have been developed and implemented on a partnership basis between government and CSOs at the grass-root and national level, to explore how partnership arrangements have been developed and implemented</li>

<li>To identify good practice in partnership arrangements and the factors which have a key influence on successful implementation of the partnerships.</li></ol> 

A compilation according to a structured research framework of examples of government/CSO collaboration.

The proposed research is to be covered all 7 oblasts of Kyrgyzstan and its capital Bishkek. The identified sample of cases and experiences relating to the cooperation between government and CSOs are geographically and functionally/methodologically representative.

</p>
      <p><strong>Type of services provided:</strong></p>
      <ul>
      <li>A compilation according to a structured research framework of examples of government/CSO collaboration</li>
		<li>A book of case studies with an introduction</li>
        <li>A list of key stakeholders to whom the book should be sent</li>
        <li>The distribution and dissemination of the book in two languages widely throughout the country</li>
      </ul>
      <p>Structured research framework is provided by creating templates of examples of government/CSO collaboration. The templates (case studies) should capture concise and general information which is used to choose methodologies and tools on which to develop a compilation of full case studies.</p> 
<p><em>Some case study categories may include: </em>
<ol><li>Basic information on the agencies running the intervention, local, sector or level or focus;</li> <li>Context and scope such as driving force behind the cooperation mechanism, the target audience or demographic group;</li> <li>Tools and Methodologies used;</li> 
<li>Results and Impact; and</li> 
<li>Further references.</li></ol>
</p>
