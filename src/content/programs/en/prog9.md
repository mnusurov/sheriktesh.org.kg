The objectives of the research:

1.  To gather and analyze data on cooperation projects and activities that have been developed and implemented on a partnership basis between government and CSOs at the grass-root and national level, to explore how partnership arrangements have been developed and implemented
2.  To identify good practice in partnership arrangements and the factors which have a key influence on successful implementation of the partnerships.

A compilation according to a structured research framework of examples of government/CSO collaboration. The proposed research is to be covered all 7 oblasts of Kyrgyzstan and its capital Bishkek. The identified sample of cases and experiences relating to the cooperation between government and CSOs are geographically and functionally/methodologically representative.

**Type of services provided:**

-   A compilation according to a structured research framework of examples of government/CSO collaboration
-   A book of case studies with an introduction
-   A list of key stakeholders to whom the book should be sent
-   The distribution and dissemination of the book in two languages widely throughout the country

Structured research framework is provided by creating templates of examples of government/CSO collaboration. The templates (case studies) should capture concise and general information which is used to choose methodologies and tools on which to develop a compilation of full case studies.

*Some case study categories may include:*

1.  Basic information on the agencies running the intervention, local, sector or level or focus;
2.  Context and scope such as driving force behind the cooperation mechanism, the target audience or demographic group;
3.  Tools and Methodologies used;
4.  Results and Impact; and
5.  Further references.
